ATS/ERS 2019 — QC criteria reference ▸
1Good start (BEV): back-extrapolated volume ≤5% FVC or ≤0.100 L, whichever is greater. BEV >0.150 L = neither acceptable nor usable.
2Hesitation time ≤2 s: time from maximal inspiration to time-zero must not exceed 2 s
3No cough in first second: cough during the 1st second invalidates FEV₁
4No early termination / glottis closure: sustained effort until EOFE; no abrupt stop or closure artifact
5Satisfactory EOFE: plateau ΔV <0.025 L in any last 1 s — or patient cannot continue and FVC ≥ largest prior FVC within repeatability tolerance. Minimum 6 s FET no longer required (2019).
6No leak: no evidence of air leak around mouthpiece
7Maximal effort throughout: no variable effort; submaximal inspiration flagged (absence of inspiratory limb alone does not fail unless extrathoracic obstruction specifically assessed)
Usable maneuver
Meets criteria 1 + 2 only (good start & hesitation ≤2 s). Even if criteria 3–7 fail. Generates Grade U if no acceptable maneuvers exist.
Repeatability thresholds
Age >6 yr: diff between 2 largest FVC <150 mL and 2 largest FEV₁ <150 mL  |  Age ≤6 yr: <100 mL or 10% of highest value, whichever greater
