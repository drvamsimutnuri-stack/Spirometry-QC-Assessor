ATS/ERS 2019 — QC criteria quick reference ▸
1Good start (BEV): ≤5% FVC or ≤0.100 L, whichever greater. BEV >0.150 L = neither acceptable nor usable.
2Hesitation time ≤2 s: from maximal inspiration to time-zero
3No cough in first second: invalidates FEV₁
4No early termination / glottis closure: sustained effort until EOFE
5Satisfactory EOFE: plateau ΔV <0.025 L in last 1 s — or cannot continue and FVC ≥ largest prior. Minimum 6 s FET no longer required.
6No leak
7Maximal effort throughout: no variable effort. Absence of inspiratory limb alone does not fail.
Usable maneuver
Meets criteria 1 + 2 only. Generates Grade U if no acceptable maneuvers exist.
